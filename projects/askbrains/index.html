What we do

AskBrains brings together leading mentors and business experts from across the globe and matches them with entrepreneurs and businesses that look for assistance. With its fast tokenized cross-border payments and affordable rates, AskBrains offers a new format of an expert network. A state-of-the-art AI scoring and matching system ensures that project teams get exactly the help they need – be it legal, cyber security, bis dev, guerrilla marketing, or finance.

The challenges

Free & budget professional networks

No expert vetting or scoring

Anyone can pretend to be an expert & offer services – including underqualified individuals and even scammers

Spam & fake accounts

Users are bombarded by dubious investment offers, promotions, & irrelevant contact requests – often from non-existing people

Difficulty connecting with experts

Experts get dozens of messages a day, so even if you find one who’s perfect for your project, you won’t get through to them

Premium networks

Exorbitant Fees

Over $1000 for a single phone call with an expert – the network keeps over 50% as its mark-up

Black Box

As an independent expert, you can’t join the network – unless it contacts you first. The fee structure is also completely opaque

No independent matching

Clients can’t choose from a catalog of experts – they have to go with the one forced on them by the network

Red Tape

Very complex paperwork and compliance procedures make accessing the services difficult in many countries
